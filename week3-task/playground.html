Flowers

Rose

Roses are one of the most popular flowers in the world. They symbolize love and beauty and come in many colors such as red, pink, white, and yellow.

Sunflower

Sunflowers are bright yellow flowers known for turning toward the sun. They represent happiness, positivity, and warmth in many cultures.

Tulip

Tulips are elegant spring flowers that grow in many vibrant colors. They are often associated with renewal, freshness, and new beginnings.
